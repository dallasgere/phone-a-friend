How to use Phone A Friend

Whats in our Phone Book?

Find a Friend

Find a friend allows you to find people who can help you with your course questions.
Just enter your course ID, Ex: CS 1428.

Become a Friend

Become a friend allows you to become a friend in our phone book for others to call.
To become a friend just enter the course ID and your contact.

Managing your Account

Transferred schools? Not a problem. We have made it easy for you to change all of your account info.
Just go to Account Settings and enter your new info.
